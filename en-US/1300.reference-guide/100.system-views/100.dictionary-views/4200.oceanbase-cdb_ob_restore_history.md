oceanbase.CDB_OB_RESTORE_HISTORY 
=====================================================



Overview 
-----------------

oceanbase.CDB_OB_RESTORE_HISTORY displays the states of completed physical restoration tasks. It is a snapshot of the records of the corresponding tasks in CDB_OB_RESTORE_PROGRESS.
