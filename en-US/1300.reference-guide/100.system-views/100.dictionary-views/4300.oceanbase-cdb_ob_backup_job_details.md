oceanbase.CDB_OB_BACKUP_JOB_DETAILS 
========================================================



Overview 
-----------------

oceanbase.CDB_OB_BACKUP_JOB_DETAILS provides detailed information about backup tasks.

--------------------------

|------------------------------|---------------|--------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
